# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 24, 2026

## Overview

MarkdownReader.org is a client-side web application that runs entirely in your browser. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.**

This application:
- Does not use cookies
- Does not use analytics or tracking
- Does not send any data to external servers
- Does not store any information about you or your usage

## How the Application Works

All processing happens locally in your browser:

- **Markdown content** you type or paste is processed entirely on your device
- **Files you upload** are read locally and never transmitted anywhere
- **PDF exports** are generated on your device and downloaded directly to your computer
- **No data** is stored after you close the browser tab

## Third-Party Services

The application loads the following resources from Content Delivery Networks (CDNs):

| Resource | Provider | Purpose |
|----------|----------|---------|
| Google Fonts | Google | Literata and JetBrains Mono fonts |
| marked.js | cdnjs (Cloudflare) | Markdown parsing library |
| html2pdf.js | cdnjs (Cloudflare) | PDF generation library |

These CDN providers may collect standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, etc.) as part of their normal operations. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacy/)

## Local Storage

This application does not use browser local storage, session storage, or IndexedDB. Your content is not persisted between sessions.

## Children's Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/maximgorbatyuk/markdown-reader/issues).

## Summary

**Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your information.**
